Name: Ajmal Kasab Crisis: 2008 Mumbai attacks Organization: Lashkar-e-Taiba Kind: Terrorist Location: Pakistan Common: External Links: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ajmal_Kasab Images: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Mohammed_Ajmal_Kasab.jpg Summary: Kasab (born 13 July 1987 died 21 November 2012) was the only attacker captured alive by police after the 2008 Mumbai attacks. He later confessed upon interrogation that the attacks were conducted with the support of Pakistan's ISI. On 29 August 2012, Kasab was found guilty of waging war and was sentenced to death by hanging by the Supreme Court of India.
