The Community First Foundation

Kind

Denver area fund-raising group

History

Founded in 1975 as a Lutheran Medical Center foundation to serve as a fund-raising section of the Medical Center a non-profit hospital. In mid-90's it participated in a two-year study to give a voice to the community when it came to health care and community programs and services. In 2007 changed their name to the Community First Foundation to reflect new mission as a community foundation to improve quality of life in the Denver area.
