Summary:
James Eagan Holmes (born December 13, 1987) is the accused perpetrator of a mass shooting that killed 12 people at a Century movie theater in Aurora, Colorado, on July 20, 2012. Holmes was known have had a very successful college career, receiving and undergraduate degree in neuroscience with highest honors, graduating in the top 1% of his class from the University of California, Riverside and pursuing a Ph.D. In 2012, his academic performance declined, receiving poor exam scores throughout his time at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign before dropping out of his studies in June 2012 and proceeding to cause the incident at Aurora, Colorado.
